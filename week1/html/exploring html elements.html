Brownie Recipe

Ingrediants

Milk
Butter
Sugar
Cocoa powder
Flour
2 eggs
Chocolate chips
Vanilla extract

Step-By-Step

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.

In a large bowl, add your softened butter and sugar together until creamy. Then mix in the milk, 2 eggs, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract. After throughly mixed, add in the flour and chocolate chips.

Pour the brownie mixure into a 9 by 12 inch pan. Spread evenly. Put in oven for 25 minutes.
