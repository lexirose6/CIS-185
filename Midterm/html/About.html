Our Mission

We love our dogs and wanted to include them in the delicous experience a bakery brings. If you can enjoy a treat, why can't they? The more dog friendly experiences there are, the better! Our mission is to use organic ingredients in our baked goods so your dogs can enjoy them. We want to offer authentic, non-proccessed foods to your pups.
